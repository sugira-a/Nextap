# Profile Studio - Company Profile Enhancement Guide

## Overview
Your Profile Studio now includes professional company profile management with auto-fill capabilities. This allows you to quickly populate templates with company and employee information.

## New Features

### 1. **Profile Data Form** 
Located in the new **Profile** tab (briefcase icon) in the left sidebar.

#### Company Information Section
- **Company Name** - Your organization name
- **Website** - Company website URL
- **Phone** - Main company contact number
- **Location** - Company address

#### Your Profile Section
- **First Name** & **Last Name** - Your full name
- **Job Title** - Your professional title/role
- **Email** - Your business email

#### Contact Links Section
- **Phone** - Your direct phone number
- **WhatsApp** - WhatsApp business number
- **Personal Website** - Your portfolio or personal site
- **Location** - Your city/region

### 2. **Photo Management**

Two professional photo uploads:

**Profile Photo**
- Your professional headshot (recommended: square format, 500x500px minimum)
- Auto-fills the first image element in templates
- Supports: JPG, PNG, WebP (max 5MB)

**Company Logo**
- Your company/brand logo
- Auto-fills the second image element in templates
- Supports: JPG, PNG, WebP, SVG (max 3MB)

### 3. **Completion Tracker**
See your profile completion status with a visual progress bar showing:
- ✅ Company Name
- ✅ First Name
- ✅ Last Name
- ✅ Job Title
- ✅ Email
- ✅ Phone
- ✅ Profile Photo

**Minimum 50% completion required** to use auto-fill feature.

### 4. **Auto-Fill Templates**
The **"Auto-fill Template"** button intelligently maps your data to template elements:

| Your Data | Maps To |
|-----------|---------|
| Full Name | Largest text (title) |
| Job Title | Medium text (subtitle) |
| Company Name | Small text (company label) |
| Profile Photo | First image |
| Company Logo | Second image |
| Phone | Phone icon row |
| Email | Mail icon row |
| Website | Globe icon row |
| Location | Map pin icon row |
| WhatsApp | Message circle icon row |

## How to Use

### Step 1: Fill Your Profile Data
1. Click the **Profile** tab (briefcase icon) in the left sidebar
2. Add your company information
3. Upload your professional photos
4. Fill in your personal details
5. Add your contact information

### Step 2: Select a Template
1. Go to the **Templates** tab
2. Choose a design that fits your style
3. The template loads into the canvas

### Step 3: Auto-Fill (Optional)
1. Go back to the **Profile** tab
2. Click **"Auto-fill Template"** button
3. All your data automatically populates the template
4. Manually adjust any fields as needed

### Step 4: Fine-tune & Save
1. Edit individual elements if desired
2. Save your design to the cloud
3. Download or share your card

## Best Practices

### Photo Quality
- **Profile Photo**: Professional headshot, good lighting, plain background
- **Logo**: Clean, recognizable, works on light & dark backgrounds
- Use consistent branding across all templates

### Template Selection
- Choose templates that complement your industry
- Light templates for formal/corporate profiles
- Dark templates for creative/modern profiles
- Consider contrast for readability

### Data Entry
- Keep titles concise (e.g., "Product Manager" not "Senior Product Manager")
- Use professional email addresses (name@company.com)
- Include country code in phone numbers (+1, +44, etc.)
- Use full URLs with https:// for websites

### Auto-Fill Best Practices
1. **Complete at least 50%** of required fields
2. **Upload high-quality photos** before auto-filling
3. **Review template layout** - some templates may have different text positions
4. **Customize after auto-fill** - adjust colors, fonts, positioning as needed

## Template Element Mapping Priority

Templates are analyzed and auto-filled based on:

1. **Text Elements** - Sorted by font size
   - Largest → Your full name
   - Medium → Job title
   - Smaller → Company name

2. **Images** - In order of appearance
   - First → Profile photo
   - Second → Company logo

3. **Icon Rows** - Matched by icon type
   - Phone → Your phone number
   - Mail → Your email
   - Globe → Website
   - MapPin → Location
   - MessageCircle → WhatsApp

## Tips & Tricks

### For Company Workspaces
- Save multiple designs with different themes
- Each design can be applied to all employee profiles
- Employees inherit company defaults from the policy

### Batch Updates
1. Update the company profile data
2. Change company logo or branding
3. Auto-fill all saved templates to apply changes throughout

### A/B Testing
- Save 2-3 different templates with the same data
- Share different versions to see which resonates better
- Use the analytics to track performance

## Troubleshooting

**"Auto-fill Template" button is disabled**
- Complete at least 50% of the profile fields
- Ensure you've added first name, last name, and email

**Photos not appearing in template**
- Template must have image elements (check Elements tab)
- Re-upload photos in correct format
- Try adjusting image position/size manually

**Data not mapping correctly**
- Different templates have different layouts
- Manually edit elements after auto-fill
- Some templates may not have all element types

**Contact information not updating**
- Verify template has icon rows for contact info
- Check that your data matches expected format
- Some templates may have custom layouts

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Tab** key - Switch between form fields
- **Enter** - Submit/confirm inputs
- **Escape** - Close dialogs

## Storage & Sync

- **Profile data is saved locally** in your browser
- **Templates are saved to cloud** with your account
- **Photos are embedded** in saved designs
- Switch devices and reload your designs
